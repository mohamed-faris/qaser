About CatchGuard

Founded in the UAE, CatchGuard is pioneering a new era of cybersecurity testing with our revolutionary pay-on-catch model.

Our Story

Born from the frustration of traditional cybersecurity consulting where organizations pay regardless of findings, CatchGuard was founded to align cybersecurity services with actual value delivery. We believe that security providers should share the risk with their clients.

Our Innovation

Our pay-on-catch model means you only pay when we find validated vulnerabilities in your systems. This revolutionary approach ensures complete alignment between our success and yours, making us true partners in your security journey.

Our Mission

To democratize enterprise-grade cybersecurity by making it accessible, affordable, and results-driven for UAE organizations of all sizes. We're committed to transforming cybersecurity from a cost center into a value-driven partnership.
